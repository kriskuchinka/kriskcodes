Meet Kris Kuchinka

Kris has lived in the Pacific Northwest for over two decades. Currently, Kris lives in Vancouver, WA with his wife and their 3 cats.


While currently working on building his portfolio and enhancing his coding skills, Kris is excited to gain real-world experience through a full time internship. He also hopes to help local non-profits with web development and design needs.


With the goal of being a life-long learner, Kris has a lot of projects and ideas on which he is focusing. His main goals in life are to develop as a human, husband, and coder.

Kris at the Hackathon

In April 2016, Kris had the opportunity to participate in Portland Startup Weekend Education (PDXSWEDU). Over the course of 48 hours, Kris and a team of 8 others worked together toward the goal of creating an education-related startup.

At the end of the weekend, the team won third place, partly because they were the only group with a working web demo. The winners were selected by a panel of judges with various experiences related to business, education and web technology. This was Kris' first "Hackathon", but he hopes it will not be his last.
